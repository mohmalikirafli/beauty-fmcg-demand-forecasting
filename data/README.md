# Data

The dataset is **synthetic** and generated by `src/generate_data.py`. It represents weekly sales for six fictional beauty products across three sales channels and three Indonesian market regions from 2024–2025. No confidential company or customer data is used.

Each row is a weekly SKU–channel–region observation. Demand incorporates controlled trend, seasonality, Ramadan/year-end uplift, promotion, marketing, channel and regional effects, plus random noise. This makes the project reproducible while preserving a realistic business setting.
